# revenue_forecast Workflow Documentation

## Overview

This workflow generates a monthly revenue forecast. It reads historical monthly actuals from `input_data.csv`, specifically the prior 24 months, to establish a baseline. A simple linear trend model is then fitted to this historical data to account for growth and implied seasonality. The model projects revenue for the next six months, and these projections, along with the actuals and a sensitivity analysis, are compiled into `monthly_revenue.xlsx`. This process is intended to run monthly as a scheduled job, or on demand when new financial actuals become available. This workflow is owned by Finance Ops and its output is consumed by the CFO for weekly reviews.
